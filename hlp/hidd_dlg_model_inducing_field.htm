Inducing Field

Specify inducing field for magnetic field computation.

Intensity. Specify the intensity of inducing field in [nT].

Inclination. Specify the inclination of inducing field in [degrees] from horizontal <-90, 90>.

Declination. Specify the declination in [degrees] clockwise from North <0, 360>.

Sample Vienna:
Intensity = 48000 nT,
Inclination = 60 degrees,
Declination = 1 degree.
